The Architecture Gallery in Munich is a platform for discussions on architecture and other related topics that involve study of space, such as photography, video, set-design and sculpture. The gallery is located in a former military bunker, with particularly striking architectural features.

In collaboration with the curator of the Architecture Gallery, Nicola Borgmann, a new corporate identity for this special place was created. The new appearance of the gallery deals with the visual representation of space and three-dimensionality. The focus is on the lighting from different sides and viewing from different angles. The concept uses a very strong formal language basic elements and builds upon it with a fine alienation of images and typography. This recalls the bold architecture of the bunker and combines it with transformative elements. It creates a strong visual representation that is highly flexible and gives the institution the opportunity to grow further.
