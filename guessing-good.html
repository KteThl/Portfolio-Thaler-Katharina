GUESSING GOOD

App Design | UI

GUESS WHAT? Good news are not boring!

GUESSING GOOD should act as a strong counterweight to the current negativity in society and thus create a new awareness of the real state and positive progress of the environment.

The quiz app uses gamification to convey fact-based, good news in an attractive and interesting way. A daily question illustrates current environmentally-based developments and acts like a concentrated dose of optimism. In addition, the user can complete different challenges, work their way up levels and unlock prizes in the process. Collected points can be discovered in the app's store and redeemed for discounts at sustainable partner brands.
